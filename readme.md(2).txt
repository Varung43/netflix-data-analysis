📺 Netflix Data Analysis Project

## 📊 Project Overview

This project explores a dataset of Netflix content to analyze trends in shows, movies, genres, and release patterns using Python libraries such as **Pandas**, **Matplotlib**, and **Seaborn**. The goal is to uncover valuable insights into Netflix’s content strategy and user preferences.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze the distribution of movies vs TV shows.
- Explore trends in content release over the years.
- Identify the most common genres and countries of production.
- Examine patterns in content duration and ratings.

---
## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

- **Python**  
- **Jupyter Notebook**  
- **Libraries:** Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn

---
📊 Key Analysis:
- Total count of Movies vs TV Shows
- Content added each year
- Top 10 content-producing countries
- Most common genres
- Top directors on Netflix

💡 Insights:
- Movies outnumber TV Shows on Netflix
- Majority of content is from the US and India
- Netflix added the most content around 2019–2020
- Drama and International Movies are the most common genres
